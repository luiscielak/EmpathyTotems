features

Empathy Totems consists of an app to track yourself , a secured data repository so you can control your data , and a physical totem structure for endless ways to express yourself .



  • Track Yourself
  • Control Your Data
  • Express Yourself


Track yourself


The data is entered through an application capturing different emotional data to be represented in correlating colors. This enables people to increase their emotional awareness level while communicating it across.
The totem's physical structure acts as an omnipresent reminder of a person's emotional climate.
My cool button

Control Your Data


The data is encrypted and secured locally.
All the data in the system is private, and you have full control over it. Sharing and exporting features are also available.

Express Yourself

The system output consists of a mystical totem serving as a visual representation of a person's emotions.
The data is represented by an LED that constantly fluctuates through multiple color patters correlated to the user's historical and current emotion data.
